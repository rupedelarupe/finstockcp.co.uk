The Finstock Community Projects group was set up in September 2006 following the success of the second annual village Fun Day. The group had previously been called the Jubilee Committee, and had organised the Jubilee party in 2002 and the playground construction in 2005. They are open to all with an interest in village matters and we are particularly keen to have representatives from the other village groups.
