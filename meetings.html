Meetings

Regular meetings take place at the Plough Inn, Finstock, and are open to all who want to get involved with the group's main project - Finstock Festival.

FCP AGM 2024

The FCP AGM 2024 will take place at 7:30pm, Wednesday 30 October 2024 at Finstock Village Hall. This is an open meeting ‐ all are welcome to attend. Once business is concluded, we will be having food (bring and share a plate) and drinks, so it will be a great opportuinity to get to know the team and what we do.
